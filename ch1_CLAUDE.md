# Chapter 1 Context — Introduction

## Purpose of This Chapter
Establish the problem, justify the study, and state objectives clearly. Reader should finish Ch1 knowing exactly what the study does, why it matters, and how it is structured.

## Core Argument
Road infrastructure is the dominant driver of LULC change in Greater Kumasi; remote sensing and GIS provide the most effective tools to quantify and spatially understand this transformation over 1995–2024.

## Research Questions (draft — confirm with supervisor)
1. How have LULC patterns in Greater Kumasi changed between 1995 and 2024?
2. How does proximity to major transport corridors influence the rate and pattern of LULC change?

## Key Points to Hit
- Establish Greater Kumasi as a fast-urbanizing tropical city
- Introduce the "Garden City" identity and its threatened status
- Justify the 1995–2024 timeframe
- State both objectives clearly and concisely
- Justify remote sensing + GIS as the appropriate methodology

## Tone
Authoritative, concise. Avoid over-explaining — the introduction sets the stage, it doesn't tell the whole story.
